"Camera Emotion Detection" leverages OpenCV to analyze real-time video feed from your device’s camera, enabling instant emotional insights.

    Real-time Analysis: Continuously processes video frames to detect faces and analyze their emotions.

    User Interaction: Enhances applications by adapting to the user’s emotional state in real-time, making interactions more responsive and engaging.

    Security Applications: Monitors environments to detect emotions such as stress or anxiety, adding an extra layer of situational awareness.
